Citcum Corporation is an international registered employees financial organization with branches globally. The organization specializes in the protection of the employee's salaries against financial loss which may result from unemployment. The organization was established to mitigate against unemployment- related financial loss. This enables financial stability for the employees and their families. Citcum Corporation is an international organization branded as having the world's largest number of employees. The organization offers "Unemployment Income" to employees who have become "unemployed" thus, maintaining the employee's financial disposition for the duration of their unemployment period.

What we do?

Citcum Corporation offers financial protection to the employees and their families against unemployment. This is sustain by weekly unemployment income paid to the employees who have become unemployed. At Citcum Corporation" we allow the employees to earn an income while unemployed."

OUR MISSION AND VISION

Citcum Corporation vision is to protect the rights of all employees while providing financial assistance to all our valuable members of employees. Our mission is to have every employee globally become a member of our organization to protect themselves and their families against loss of income that may arise as a rendition of unemployment.

WHY WORK FOR US

We provide a platform of growth and development with unmatchable employee's benefits and compensation packages. At Citcum Corporation our employee's salaries are the highest in the labour industry and our employees enjoy an environment that is conducive to growth and self development. We give our employees the freedom to set their own goals and to exceed their own expectations. Each employee is part of our organization's decision making process and are included in the organization's development. Citcum Corporation is the most desirable place for employees to work.
